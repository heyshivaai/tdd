The Market Opportunity

Crosslake's expertise can now serve every deal — not just the ones that fit the calendar.

Technology due diligence today is built around the practitioner — expert by expert, engagement by engagement. Crosslake has built an exceptional practice on this model, completing 6,000+ engagements and developing deep pattern recognition across every major tech vertical.

The next stage of growth is about multiplying that expertise at scale. An agentic AI workforce handles the systematic analysis — data gathering, cross-referencing, pattern matching, structured documentation — while Crosslake's practitioners and partners focus on what they do best: judgment, client relationships, and deal structuring.

The result is a practice that can serve more deals, faster, at lower cost — and that gets smarter with every engagement through a proprietary signal corpus that compounds over time.

Three Ways This Creates Value
⚡
First
The Intake & Baseline Engine
Fastest to deploy · Immediate win
Every engagement starts with the same systematic work — collecting documentation, reviewing repositories, scanning for signals, structuring findings. AI staff own this entirely. A standardized intake workflow ingests whatever a target provides — codebase access, architecture docs, vendor contracts, org charts, management questionnaires — and produces a structured first-pass assessment benchmarked against industry data. What used to take a practitioner a week of oriented reading now takes hours, freeing them for the work that requires human judgment.

The Practitioner Multiplier
Protects relationships · Expands capacity
Rather than changing the practitioner model, AI staff become what each practitioner gets paired with. Practitioners become more valuable, more available, and more central to every engagement — with AI amplifying their impact rather than replacing their role.

Every new engagement feeds it. Over time, this is the asset that defines the franchise value.
